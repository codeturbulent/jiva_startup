Reading, Reimagined.

Jiva is an AI-powered companion that transforms how you interact with documents. We turn static PDFs into dynamic, voice-controlled conversations.

Our Mission

We believe knowledge shouldn't be locked behind tedious scrolling. Our mission is to make information accessible, interactive, and effortless.

Whether you are a student crunching through research papers or a professional reviewing contracts, Jiva gives your documents a voice.

The Story

Jiva started with a simple frustration: reading on screens is exhausting. We combined the power of Large Language Models (LLMs) with intuitive voice controls to solve this.
